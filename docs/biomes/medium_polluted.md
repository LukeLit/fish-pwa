# Medium Polluted Biome: Toxic Depths

## Overview

**Biome ID**: `medium_polluted`  
**Name**: Toxic Depths  
**Base Depth**: Medium (50-200m)  
**Modifiers**: Polluted  
**Visual Theme**: Dark, murky waters with toxic waste and mutated life

## Essence Types

- **Primary**: Shallow, Polluted
- **Secondary**: None

## Unlock Requirements

- **Shallow Essence**: 150
- **Polluted Essence**: 75
